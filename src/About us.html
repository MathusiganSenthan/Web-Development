HISTORY



Established in 2020, Lanka PropertyPro is currently Sri Lanka's most visited property and apartment listing website with over 1000 active Sri Lankan ads in the market. Featuring lands, houses, apartments for both rent and sale in all four corners of the island, LankaPropertyPro.com is open to anyone to submit their property for sale or rent.Our website was started with the intention of providing an online platform for property owners and agents in Sri Lanka to advertise their property. Our aim is to become the online property portal of choice for all Sri Lankans, where agents will find buyers to make their property buying and selling process easy and effective.


We continuously work hard to improve our service and have invested heavily to provide the latest technology and features available to our advertisers and buyers to make their property buying and selling process easy and effective.
